Spacing

Learn how to change the space of the elements with Astra.

Margins and paddings

To add margin or padding to an element, there are a lot of classes that are the result of a combination. These classes are formed by: m or p, followed by the direction and the size. For example, a minimum margin at the top will be the m-top-1 class, and a general padding with mid distance will be p-3.

m-top-2
p-x-5
m-y-3
m-bottom-5 p-left-5

Width and height

Changing the width and height of an element can be possible with Astra with a limited possibilities. With the w-full or h-full the width or height will be the 100%, and 50% with w-half or h-half. You can also use the w-screen or w-screen, and it will expand to the size of the screen. For more small measures, you can use w-1 to w-5 or h-1 to h-5 classes.
